About the  Department

Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one among the leading and oldest departments of Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology. The department is reputed for its excellence in teaching and training engineering students for generations since 1987. The department caters Under Graduate, Post Graduate and Ph.D. courses in all the specialised areas of computing. The CSE has pioneered in setting education standards par excellence that can be sensed by the presence of alumni in all the leading Multinational software firms like Microsoft, Infosys, Oracle, Verizon, Virtusa, Cognizant, Wipro etc. The department is enriched with well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty members. Endowed with futuristic vision of generating sustained quality manpower for the ever-increasing global demand of software firms, the CSE is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to provide excellent and live training for the budding engineers. These facilities include collaborative research labs from Virtusa and CISCO with cutting-edge technologies and software that are housed in more than 10000 sq.ft buildings with multiple floors. The organisational hierarchy for smooth dissemination of teaching and research operations in the department is made effective by 96 committed faculties and 15 technical support staff. 

We have 42 doctorates in our department and their research specialisation include Data Mining, Software engineering, Mobile Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, ,Internet of Things,Cyber Security,Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Web Services, Computational Intelligence and Big data. Some highlights of the research activities carried out by faculty members of department of CSE include, Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) funded data mining projects, on-board computer development for Sathyabama NanoSatellite SATHYABAMASAT (done by students and staff), nearly 7 funded projects from Department of Science and Technology (DST) on various discipline. Staff and student are publishing their research articles regularly in high impact journals. The department is having more than 15 patents and a very good citation index. Recently department of CSE received fund from DST-FIST to setup WSN and IOT Lab and Cloud Lab. The student projects are well coordinated to cultivate creative thinking and practical problem solving abilities among students through Industry-Institute collaboration efforts. This includes various campus development student projects like BHAVANI from government institutes like BARC. The measure of employability among students after immediate engineering graduation is the visual sign of healthy growth of any educational institution. In this regard, department of computer science and engineering has always stood in distinction with maximum numbers of student recruited by top IT firms through campus placements. Further, the entrepreneurship capabilities of students are constantly revived through departmental workshops and research conferences, which are conducted periodically in collaboration with leading contemporary managerial gurus. 

With the widespread deployment of Computers in all the fields, the demand for the Computer Engineer is on a high note and provides a very good job opportunity in various domains. The major domains in which the IT plays a major role include Business Data Maintenance, Business Communication, Finance Sector (banking and insurance), Manufacturing and Services Industry, Defense, Industrial Control, Medical, Aerospace, Automobile, Telecommunications, Data Analysis, Scientific and Industrial Research and much more. There is always a rise in the requirement of Computer Professionals globally. 

Multi-national organizations that recruit Computer Professionals includes Google, Yahoo, Microsoft, Oracle Corporation, IBM, Infosys Technologies, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Cognizant Technology Solutions (CTS), Wipro Technologies, Accenture, HCL, iGATE, Caritor, CSC, Hexaware Technologies, Zoho Corp, L&T Infotech, Mahindra Satyam, Syntel, iNautix, Patni Computers, and Virtusa, among others. There is also a lot of demand from the Mobile Industry giants like Samsung, Apple, Nokia, etc. BSNL, CDAC, NIC, ISRO, IGCAR, etc., are some of the Government Organizations that recruit Information Technology professionals. There are several Research and Development Organizations (both Government and Private) that require the services of an Computer professional. The starting salary ranges from 3.5 Lakhs to 18 Lakhs per annum. The Computer professionals can also pursue higher studies and research in India and abroad. The students can also become entrepreneurs without any major investment. There is a lot of scope for free lancers and they can earn huge amount of money.
